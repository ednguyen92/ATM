About Us

Advanced Tech Machining LLC is a precision machine shop with state-of-the-art CNC machining centers and the most experienced machinists and programmers in Fremont, California. We have over more than 30 years of experience working in the machining industry. Here at Advanced Tech Machining, we make metal and plastic machine parts for a variety of local industries. Our parts are manufactured using the finest equipment and decades of experience. We offer competitive pricing and we work quickly and efficiently
