Capabilities

Welding

We are proficient in several welding techniques and lathe machines to produce perfect metalwork. We can easily and correctly handle complex and time-sensitive jobs. Our prices are reasonable and won’t drain your savings.

Sheet Metalworking

Any need for sheet metal fabrication our machine and metal shop can also provide. We can produce sheet metal for commercial, industrial, residential buildings and whatever unique cases our clients could have.

R&D Prototyping

Clients may have the need to be developing something innovative and that requires custom work and a high level of understanding from the metalworker. We provide keen services to work with clients on creating the perfect prototype design so that it may hit production as soon as possible.

Production Components

Large companies with large scale production lines can come to Advanced Tech Machining when they need support in creating parts for proprietary machinery. We can produce several essential components for machines in the Aerospace, Medical, Automotive and Semiconductor industries.

CNC Lathe & Milling

At Advanced Tech Machining LLC, we can manufacture various metal, wood, plastic, and ceramic products and parts. We can also fabricate elements made of composites. We work in harmony with the industry’s most elevated standards and we are certain that you will be beyond happy with our services.
